Players Spotlights

Featured Player of the Week: Luke Kuechly

Luke Kuechly, known as "Captain America" to Panthers fans, is a legendary figure in Carolina Panthers history. A first-round draft pick in 2012, Kuechly wasted no time making his mark on the NFL, earning Defensive Rookie of the Year honors in his debut season. His unparalleled instincts, football IQ, and sideline-to-sideline dominance quickly established him as one of the premier linebackers in the league. Throughout his career, Kuechly's leadership and playmaking ability were instrumental in the Panthers' success, earning him numerous accolades including seven Pro Bowl selections and five First-Team All-Pro honors. His crowning achievement came in 2013 when he was named NFL Defensive Player of the Year after recording a league-leading 153 tackles, four interceptions, and two touchdowns. Off the field, Kuechly's humility, work ethic, and dedication to the community endeared him to fans and teammates alike. His charitable endeavors, including his involvement with the "Kuechly's Crew" program, showcased his commitment to making a positive impact beyond the game. Despite retiring prematurely in 2020 due to concerns over concussions, Kuechly's legacy as one of the greatest Panthers of all time remains firmly intact. His contributions to the franchise and the game of football will be celebrated for years to come, serving as an inspiration to future generations of Panthers players and fans alike.

Other Fearured Players

Taylor Moton

Position: Offensive Tackle Taylor Moton is a stalwart offensive tackle known for his consistency, strength, and durability. Drafted by the Carolina Panthers in the second round of the 2017 NFL Draft, Moton has become a cornerstone of the team's offensive line. His ability to protect the quarterback's blindside and pave the way for the running game has earned him recognition as one of the top offensive tackles in the league. Moton's reliability and leadership make him an integral part of the Panthers' offensive unit.

Shaq Thompson

Position: Linebacker Shaq Thompson is a versatile linebacker known for his speed, athleticism, and ability to make plays all over the field. Drafted by the Carolina Panthers in the first round of the 2015 NFL Draft, Thompson has emerged as a leader on the team's defense. His combination of coverage skills, run-stopping ability, and instincts makes him a valuable asset to the Panthers' defense. In the 2021 season, Thompson recorded over 100 tackles, showcasing his ability to consistently make an impact on the field.

Chubba Hubbard

Position: Running Back Chuba Hubbard is a dynamic running back known for his speed, elusiveness, and big-play ability. Drafted by the Carolina Panthers in the fourth round of the 2021 NFL Draft, Hubbard has quickly made an impact on the team's offense. His explosive burst through the hole, vision, and ability to break tackles make him a threat to score every time he touches the ball. In his rookie season, Hubbard showcased his talent by providing a spark to the Panthers' rushing attack and contributing as a receiver out of the backfield.
